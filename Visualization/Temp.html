Latitude vs. Temperature

The figure shows that there is a correlation between latitude and temperature. As the data moves away from the equator (0),
a significant drop in temperature occurs. The reason for this could be because the eqautor is closer to the sun, so the heat from the sun
causes the high temperature. The data that is far from the eqautor has lower temperatures because it moves away from being close to sun. If
someone wants to live in a warm environment, they could use the chart to determine that living at or near the equator would be best.
